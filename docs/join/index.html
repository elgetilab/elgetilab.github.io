Open positions

Graduate student. Are you interested in protein design, hyperpolarization, and the development of new molecular sensors? Join Hyp*Mol, a new, collaborative, and interdisciplinary research program funded by the German Research Foundation. The open position is for a graduate (PhD) student, who will be mentored jointly by three PIs from Leipzig University (Matthias Elgeti, Irene Coin, and Jens Meiler. The successful candidate will apply cutting edge biochemical, spectroscopic and computational methods to design, optimize and integrate novel “Maquette” proteins. Please contact Matthias here for further information and to apply.
